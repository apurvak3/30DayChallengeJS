"The Tantric Curse" by Anupama Garg is a novel that delves into the world of tantra, mysticism, and self-discovery. The story centers around a young girl named Rhea, who is drawn into the mystical and often misunderstood realm of tantra. Guided by her guru, Anu, Rhea embarks on a journey of spiritual awakening, self-discovery, and empowerment.
